# Moontrack

> Your cycle. Understood.

A cycle tracker that respects your privacy and actually gives useful coaching — for energy, sleep, and training.

## What you get

- **Predictions that adapt** — Not a rigid 28-day model — learns your actual pattern, including irregular ones.
- **Training in sync** — Schedule heavy lifts, long runs, recovery days with your cycle, not against it.
- **Health data stays here** — Stored on your phone. Never sold. Opt-out of cloud sync entirely if you want.

## Category

Health. Part of a 50-product exploration of high-demand consumer and SMB markets.
